Stock comparison FAQ

How do I compare two stocks on one animated chart?

In Animate Stock, create a project and add two (or more) tickers. The app automatically finds the date range where their histories overlap and animates both lines on a single chart, each with its own color.

How many stocks can I compare at once?

You can add many tickers to one chart — comparisons with 8 or more lines work smoothly. Each line gets a distinct color you can customize.

Can I compare a stock against an index or crypto?

Yes. You can mix asset types freely — for example Tesla vs the S&P 500, or Nvidia vs Bitcoin. Anything with historical price data can go on the same chart.

What happens if the stocks have different listing dates?

The app detects the overlapping period automatically and validates it before creating the project, so the comparison is always apples-to-apples over a shared timeframe.

Can I export the comparison as a GIF?

Yes — export as a looping GIF for feeds and threads, or as a high-quality MP4 for YouTube and TikTok.
