What-if investment FAQ

How does the “what if I invested” feature work?

Pick a stock, a starting amount (like $100 or $1,000), and a purchase date in the past. Animate Stock then animates how the value of that investment evolved month by month all the way to today — including every crash and rally along the way.

What if I bought $100 of Nokia in 2008?

That’s exactly the kind of scenario the app is built for. Set the ticker to NOK, the amount to $100, and the date to 2008 — then watch the animation show what that investment would be worth at every point since, for better or worse.

Can I run scenarios on crypto or ETFs?

Yes. What-if scenarios work on anything with historical price data: individual stocks, ETFs like SPY or QQQ, indices, and major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin.

How accurate are the results?

Calculations use adjusted historical closing prices, which account for stock splits. Results are illustrative — they don’t include dividends reinvestment timing, fees, or taxes — and are not investment advice.

Can I share the result as a video?

Yes — that’s the point! Export the scenario as a watermark-free MP4 or looping GIF, perfect for “what if” posts that get people talking.
