Frequently asked questions

What is Animate Stock?

Animate Stock is an iPhone app that turns historical stock market data into animated chart videos and GIFs. Pick a ticker and a time range, customize the look, and export a share-ready MP4 or GIF in minutes.

Which stocks and assets are supported?

Anything with public market data: US and international stocks, ETFs, indices, and major cryptocurrencies. If a ticker has historical price data, you can animate it.

What export formats are available?

You can export high-quality MP4 video (1440x1080 landscape or 1080x1080 square, 30 fps) or an animated GIF that loops forever — perfect for X, Reddit, TikTok, YouTube, and newsletters.

Do my videos have a watermark?

No. Every video and GIF you export is clean and watermark-free, with full control over colors, title, duration, and aspect ratio.

How far back does the historical data go?

As far back as data exists for each ticker — for many large-cap stocks that means 20+ years of price history. The app shows you the available date range for every ticker you add.
