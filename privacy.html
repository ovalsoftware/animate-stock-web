Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This policy describes how Animate Stock (the iOS app) and animatestock.com (the website), operated by Applorium, handle your information.

What the app collects

Animate Stock does not require an account and does not collect personal information. Your projects, custom events, and exported videos are stored locally on your device.

Market data requests

When you search for a ticker or load price history, the app requests data from third-party market data providers. These requests include the ticker symbol and date range but no personal identifiers beyond what any internet request technically includes (such as your IP address, handled by the provider under their own policies).

Photos access

If you choose to save an exported video or GIF, the app asks for permission to add it to your photo library. The app only writes the files you export; it does not read your existing photos.

The website

animatestock.com is a static website. It does not set cookies and does not run third-party trackers.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
