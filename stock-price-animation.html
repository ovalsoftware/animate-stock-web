Stock price animation FAQ

How do I make an animated stock chart video?

Open Animate Stock on your iPhone, search for a ticker, pick a date range, and tap export. The app renders a smooth animated line chart of the price history and saves it as an MP4 video or GIF — no editing skills or desktop software needed.

Can I animate any time period?

Yes. You can animate anything from a few months to the entire trading history of a stock — for many companies that is 20+ years of data. The app shows the earliest available date for every ticker.

Can I mark events like earnings or crashes on the chart?

Yes. You can add event markers with a custom title, date, and color — for example “iPhone launch”, “COVID crash”, or “stock split”. Events you create are saved to a global library so you can reuse them across projects.

How long can the animation be?

You choose the duration: presets from 5 to 90 seconds, or set a custom length. Short loops work great as GIFs; longer renders suit YouTube and TikTok narration.

Can I customize how the chart looks?

Yes. Every MP4 and GIF exports with your choice of background color, light or dark theme, line color, and chart title.
